Introduction to Web Development

House of Web

Welcome to the thrilling House of Web, a minigame that concludes our Introduction to Web Development lesson. You'll navigate through a seemingly infinite number of floors, each randomly generated to test your knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. As you progress through the House of Web, you'll encounter different floors, each representing one of the three topics. Get ready to face challenging questions related to HTML elements, CSS styling, and JavaScript concepts. But don't worry, it's all in good fun!

Here's how it works: Once you enter a floor, a question will appear on the screen. Use your web development knowledge to answer correctly. If you get it right, you'll proceed to the next level, advancing further into the digital house. However, beware! If you answer incorrectly, you'll be dejected back to the start, just like in any quest. But don't let that discourage you; the House of Web is meant to challenge you, and it's a great opportunity to reinforce what you've learned.

In total, there are 15 floors to conquer, so stay sharp and focused as you navigate through the halls of web development. Remember, every correct answer brings you closer to the end of the game, where you'll emerge as a confident and skilled web developer.

Have fun exploring the House of Web, and don't forget to face each question with determination. You've got this!
