Principles of Computer Science

Learn programming fundamentals, problem-solving with algorithms, and the art of logical thinking. Engage in hands-on projects, exploring web development, data science, and more. Whether you're a novice or tech enthusiast, join us on this transformative journey into the digital realm. Unlock a world of possibilities in computer science and coding!
This course has 13 lessons that cover basic computer science concepts, each with videos, articles, and minigames to help reinforce the topics discussed. After completing all lessons the Mastery Quiz becomes available, which is requires a passing grade to unlock the final project. Successfully complete the final project to earn 3 Celestial Credits for this course!
